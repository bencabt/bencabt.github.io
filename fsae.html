UPenn FSAE

As a member of the UPenn FSAE team, I have had the opportunity to build and design an actuated steering system as well as redesign the braking system of the University of Pennsylvania's Electric Racing vehicle.

Goals of the braking system investigation include:

  • Minimize friction within braking system and lock wheels ONLY towards the end of brake pedal stroke
  • Maximize regenerative braking
  • Make brake bias adjustment easier
  • Quantitatively understand exact position
  • Facilitate brake bleeding
